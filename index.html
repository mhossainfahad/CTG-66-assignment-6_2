The Concept

Lendino is a marketplace for loans where private and professional investors can lend directly to creditworthy businesses.

Local Loan

Lendino's loan amrket enables loans between companies and investors with local knowledge. Lendino thus strangthening local ties.

Better Financing

Lendino allows companies to borrow directly from Danish investors. This gives higher returns to lenders and lower costs for borrowers.
